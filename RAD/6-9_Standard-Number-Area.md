# 6.9 STANDARD NUMBER AREA

## 6.9A. Preliminary rule

### 6.9A1. Scope. 
Use this area only to item level description to record international numbers pertaining to the item, including published architectural series and files described at the item levels.

### 6.9A2. Punctuation
Precede this area by a full stop, space, dash, space or start a new paragraph.
