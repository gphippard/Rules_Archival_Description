## 5.9A. Preliminary rule[^40]

### 5.9A1. Scope. 
Use this area only in item level description to record international numbers pertaining to the item, including published cartographic series and files described at the item level.

### 5.9A2. Punctuation
Precede this area by a full stop, space, dash, space or start a new paragraph.
