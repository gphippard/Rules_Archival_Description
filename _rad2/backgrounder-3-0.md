This section suggests areas for the archival community to consider when thinking about the impact of a revised descriptive standard.
+ Training and education
+ Systems and software
+ Funding
+ Policy development
+ Planning
+ Research

This section does not attempt to answer the question of impact in these areas – each archivist and/or institution will have to assess the impact based on their own situation.

The process and timeline for review of RAD2 by the Canadian archival community is outlined below.

|&nbsp; | &nbsp; |
| --- | --- |
| January 2004 | RAD2 discussion draft document made available for review |
| March 2004 | Deadline for community feedback on RAD2 discussion draft |
| Spring 2004 | Compendium of comments reviewed by CCAD |
| Spring 2004 | Interim findings reported to the community at national and/or provincial archival association conferences |
| Autumn 2004 | RAD2 Report, with recommendations for next steps |
